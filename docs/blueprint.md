# **App Name**: AstroSage

## Core Features:

- Daily Horoscopes: Provide daily horoscopes based on the user's zodiac sign.
- Birth Chart Generation: Generate a personal birth chart based on the user's birth date, time, and location.
- Birth Chart Interpretation: Use AI to interpret the user's birth chart, providing insights into their personality, strengths, and weaknesses. The AI tool should provide relevant information based on the planets in the houses.
- Zodiac Compatibility: Check compatibility between two zodiac signs, providing a detailed analysis of their potential relationship.
- Planetary Influences: Explain the influence of different planets on the user's life and destiny.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep purple (#4B0082) to evoke a sense of mystery and spirituality.
- Background color: Light purple (#E6E6FA) to complement the deep purple without being overpowering.
- Accent color: Gold (#FFD700) for highlights, text, and icons to symbolize wisdom and cosmic energy.
- Body and headline font: 'Alegreya', a humanist serif font, with an elegant, intellectual, contemporary feel. 
- Use elegant, celestial-themed icons in gold to represent planets, zodiac signs, and astrological aspects.
- Subtle animations such as stars twinkling or planets orbiting to create a magical and immersive experience.
